The Story Of My Life

Amelia K. Bujar was born in Koszalin, Poland, in 2002 and moved to Bergen, Norway, in 2006. I have always had a passion for technology, accumulating skills in photography, videography, 3D animation, marketing, content creation, and front-end development.

My front-end development journey began in August 2023 at Noroff Bergen, where they plan to graduate in June 2025. During my second year, I started freelancing to gain practical experience.

Prior to that, I studied 3D animation at Noroff Oslo from August 2022 to June 2023, where I developed skills in animation but found my main passion in front-end development.

From 2018 to 2022, I completed a "Full-Time Filmmaker" course by Parker Walberg, gaining experience in videography and photography. Additionally, in June 2020, I launched a literature-focused website called “The Book Corner Chronicles,” where I discuss literature, interview authors, and review books.
